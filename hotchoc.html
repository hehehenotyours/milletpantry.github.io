Ragi Hot Chocolate Mix


Rs. 95

Net Weight: 100g

Ingredients: Ragi Flour, Thread Mishri, Milk Powder, Cocoa Powder.

Allergen Information: Contains milk solids.

Mode of Preparation: Add 1-2 tablespoons of the ragi hot chocolate mix to a cup. Pour in 1 cup of hot milk (or a milk alternative) and stir well until the mix dissolves completely. Adjust sweetness to taste by adding more thread mishri or natural sweetener if needed.

Shelf Life: 2 months
